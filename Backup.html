About Alberto Watch Company

Alberto Watch Company has been a trusted name in luxury timepieces for over 30 years. We pride ourselves on offering exceptional quality, unparalleled service, and expert knowledge in the world of fine watches.

Our History

Founded in 1990 by Alberto Rossi, our company began as a small watch repair shop in the heart of the city. Alberto's passion for horology and commitment to excellence quickly earned him a reputation among watch enthusiasts and collectors.

Our Mission

At Alberto Watch Company, our mission is to provide our customers with the finest timepieces and exceptional service. We believe that a watch is more than just a timekeeping device—it's an expression of personal style, a family heirloom, and a work of art.

Our Expertise

Our team of certified watchmakers and sales professionals undergo rigorous training to ensure they can provide expert advice and service. Whether you're purchasing your first luxury watch or adding to an extensive collection, we're here to guide you through the process.
